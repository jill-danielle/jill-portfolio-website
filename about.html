About Me

I am a UX designer who has a background of Fine Arts in Design and currently working for my family's plastic manufacturing business as an Office Administrator. When I first started to work in this business I had no background in manufacturing but as time goes by and through daily experience and challenges, I have learned the importance of having soft skills and an inner drive in identifying and solving problems within the company.

While working, I studied UX Design on my free time and have created a conceptual app built individually from start to finish using UX processes, a hi-fidelity prototype and a website showcasing the Design Thinking Process on how I built the app as my first output.

As a UX Designer, my goal is to passionately seek insights from data research and to use the analyzed data to create simple and thoughtful solutions for users.

I am also currently studying Frontend Development to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build a website from scratch with the understanding of testing and debugging the website.
